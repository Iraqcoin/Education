About The Professor

Tim Dikun

Tim is a designer from Seattle who started designing for the web before he could drive and when nested tables were still cool. Working for Envy Labs, he loves designing products that balance beauty and usability and believes that enjoying life's offerings beyond the internet is one of the best professional development tools there is.

Course Reviews (9)

abraunton

I found this course very easy to follow and informative, it covers the fundamentals but you will still learn a lot of things that you generally don't see in everyday tutorials. The best part for me was the color section, I have always struggled with colors but now I understand how colors can compliment each other and how to achieve that. I hope there will be a follow up course!

MaskellRyan

I have learned mostly all of this information before in school; but never was the fundamentals of design displayed, taught, or as interactive as it was in this course! I am thoroughly pleased and even delighted with the experience of re-learning these core concepts, and I fully recommend this course to other beginners, or those with experience.

Hipsterbrown

This is definitely a new area of education for Code School, and they have done a fantastic job on this fundamentals course. They cover the basics and spark further curiosity into what goes into making a great web design. You will never look at font the same again, and every color scheme you see around the web will make you wonder what kind of motivation went into creating it. Even if design is not in your job description, this is the perfect course to understand what a designer is thinking and will help improve dev/designer relations overall. I can't wait to see what they come up with next!

happyjew

I really enjoyed Fundamentals of Design, the information was practical and very informative, even for someone who's done design work before. I love CS's approach, the videos rocked as always and the challenges instructive and insightful.
